MYRDALE
Photo by John Doe on Unsplash

The Myrdale is Toronto's first and largest private ranch offering year-round activities including horseback riding, tennis, walking trails, snowshoeing, and indoor golf simulators. With over 200 acres of land, the Myrdale remains a place where families and friends can enrich their lives, spend valuable time together and share memorable moments.
